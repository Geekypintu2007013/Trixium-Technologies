Our Products

Electronics

We design and develop production-ready electronics from concept to manufacture. Services include schematic capture, PCB layout, component selection, embedded firmware, sensor integration, and thermal/EMC validation. We focus on rugged, low-power designs and DFM-ready layouts to accelerate time-to-market and simplify certification and scaling for volume production.

Drones

End-to-end UAV development for autonomous and semi-autonomous platforms. We build flight controllers, autopilots, navigation and sensor fusion stacks, payload integration (imaging, LiDAR, inspection), and flight testing workflows. Emphasis on reliability, safety, and regulatory compliance for commercial and research deployments.

Edutech

Hands-on learning products and curricula for electronics, embedded systems, and robotics. We create modular kits, simulation tools, instructor guides, and lab exercises that teach circuit design, microcontroller programming, control systems, and systems integration — ideal for universities, training centers, and STEM outreach programs.

Defence & R&D

Secure, ruggedized systems and collaborative R&D services for defense and advanced projects. Capabilities include rapid prototyping, environmental hardening, secure firmware/boot strategies, systems engineering, and field validation to mature concepts through TRL milestones toward operational deployment.
